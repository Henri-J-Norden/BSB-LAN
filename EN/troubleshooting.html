Troubleshooting

BSB-LAN tries to make accessing your heating system as easy as possible, but there are always things that can go wrong. If you still have a problem after reading this document as well as the FAQ, please open up a bug report on the project's GitHub page and make sure that you provide us with the necessary log files, especially from the Serial Monitor (Tools/Serial Monitor in the Arduino IDE). Make sure you copy the messages right from the moment your microcontroller boots until the moment you encounter the problem. Please do not send screenshots, only plain text files.
